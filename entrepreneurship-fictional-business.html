Our location

We will be located locally, in Plaquemine. Our only requirements for a location are proximity to residence, good internet, and availability of performance computer parts. Because of the internet, anyone can buy anything from anywhere; and allows us to be located anywhere. We do not need performant internet because our business wouldn't perceive any benefit and it would be an unnecessary expense. Therefore, we could operate our business (almost) anywhere with a good internet infastructure. Which leaves us to one final requirement: proximity to residence. We have options for decent internet in Plaquemine, and we live in close proximity, so it would be most reasonable to operate locally.


Competition

I do not anticipate having any serious competition. There is no business within 15 miles of our chosen location that would be serious competition. However, there is a locally owned RadioShack in town, but I do no anticipate serious competition from them because we provide a broader range of products that we make ourselves, while Radio Shack is just a small retail store.
