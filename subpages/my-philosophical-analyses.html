Are the wheels on the car, or is the car on the wheels?

Here me out, if the house is on stilts, the dog is on the chair, and so on, then why are the wheels on the car? The arguement of the car being the largest object and therefore, the wheels are on the car can't hold any ground. Again, the house is on stilts, not the stilts are on the house, the dog is on the chair, not the chair is on the dog. If the chair were on the dog, that would imply that the chair is ontop, but if the wheels are on the car, wouldn't they be ontop too?
