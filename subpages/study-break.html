Study Break


What is Study Break?

Study Break is a program I made to help me and others study. While there are other options out there, this one's probably one of a few made by a 10th grader in his spare time at school. Plus, Study Break may be the first web-based option created in Scratch and hosted on a dedicated website. Sure the other options may be better, but Study Break is unique in many ways.




Why did I make Study Break?

I created Study Break because I find that I learn best by taking actual tests. I have used other web-based studying programs, but I wanted to make a studying program that I can call my own. So, Study Break was born and it can be used by anyone and accessed from anywhere where internet roams.




Where can I use Study Break?

Study Break goes everywhere the internet wanders. Study Break current resides at https://study-break.github.io.



How was development?

Study Break originally just a program that I created over the course of roughly a total of 10 hours. However, development was spread throughout several days and I only worked on it during my free time at school. I didn't originally intend for it to become a website when I started creating the program, but I decided to make it into a website so it's more accessible. I created Study Break in Scratch using the TurboWarp editor and packager.
