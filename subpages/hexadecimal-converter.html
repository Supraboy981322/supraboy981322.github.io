Hexadecimal Number Converter



What is this?

This is a hexadecimal number translator to convert a hexidecimal value to a base 10 value.




How do I use this?

You put in a hexadecimal value and press "= Convert"




Convert from Hexadecimal




Please Note

Please do not press the "enter" on your keyboard, it will reset the input box

If a "#" is appended infront of the hexadecimal value, it will not work
