Local Video Player


What is this?

This is a video player that runs right in your browser and allows you to watch your own video files from the convinence of your web browser. None of your video files are transfered over the internet from this webpage.




Can I download this?

Yes, you can download this as an HTML file here. This file will open in your web browser like a website, but it'll be 100% local and won't require an internet connection to access, once downloaded.
