Local Audio Player


What is this?

This is a audio player that runs right in your browser and allows you to listen to your own audio files from the convinence of your web browser. None of your audio files are transfered over the internet from this webpage.




Can I download this?

Yes, you can download this as an HTML file here. This file will open in your web browser like a website, but it'll be 100% local and won't require an internet connection to access, once downloaded.
