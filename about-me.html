About me


Hi, I'm Zane Sanders, a 16-year-old from the USA. I'm interested in math, science, drawing, programming, and game development using Scratch. I'm also autistic and have ADHD. People often tell me I'm smart and could achieve great things, but I'm not sure what "great things" could be for me. Some (including my teachers) compare me to Einstein, Bill Gates, Steve Jobs, and Paul Allen. I've even been told I remind them of Elon Musk's teenage years. I have lots of ideas, but I struggle to write them down before forgetting. I honestly don't know what I want for my future because I enjoy all aspects of technology. Currently, I'm leaning towards web development, but I also like hardware engineering.
